Run it in the Dock, not just the menu bar

WhatPort can now live in the Dock as a normal windowed app, if the menu bar isn't your thing. Flip "Show in Dock" in Settings and switch back whenever you like.

## New

- **Dock and window mode.** Turn on "Show in Dock" in Settings and WhatPort opens in a standard window with a Dock icon, instead of living in the menu bar. Turn it off to go back. Your choice sticks across launches.

## Improvements

- **Buy a licence without leaving the app.** The Pro unlock screen now links straight to checkout, so finding where to buy a key isn't a treasure hunt.
- **Tidied the Pro unlock screen.** Dropped a "Restore Purchases" button that did nothing on the direct download.

## Install

Download below, unzip, and drag WhatPort.app to Applications, or:

```
brew upgrade --cask whatport
```

The app is signed and notarised by Apple.
