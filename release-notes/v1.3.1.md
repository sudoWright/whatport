Know why your Mac isn't charging

When a charger is plugged in but your Mac isn't charging, WhatPort now tells you why instead of leaving you guessing.

## New

- **Charging status.** Open a charger's port and WhatPort shows its state: Charging, Fully charged, On hold to protect battery health (Optimized Battery Charging or an 80% limit), or simply Plugged in, not charging. It only reports a reason it can actually confirm, never a guess.

## Install

Download below, unzip, and drag WhatPort.app to Applications, or:

```
brew upgrade --cask whatport
```

The app is signed and notarised by Apple.
